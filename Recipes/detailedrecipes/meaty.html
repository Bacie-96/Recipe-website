Fried chicken

  • 3 cups of milk

  • 2 beaten eggs
  • 1 tsp of garlic powder, tumeric, black pepper, paprika & salt

  • Chicken pieces boiled
  • corn flour and water

Instructions

  1. n a bowl add milk
  2. Add vinegar, eggs, and all the spices
  3. Add a cup of flour and mix
  4. Put the chicken in the mixture and refrigirate over the night
  5. In a seperate bowl add 2 cups of flour and all the spices tsp each
  6. Add chicken to the flour, then to water bowl and back to flour
  7. Deep fry until golden brown

Beef

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Beef
  • Onions,Carrots, Potatoes, Garlic
  • Salt, pepper, Bay leaves, Thyme
  • Tomato paste, Worcesterine sauce
  • Beef brooth (mix water & beef stock)

Instructions

  1. Season beef with salt, pepper and sprinkle flour and coat all sides
  2. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat
  3. Brown beefon all sides and transfer beef to bowl
  4. Add onion, garlic, and Carrots, cook until golden brown
  5. Add Potatoes, beef broth, tomato paste, bay leaves, Thyme and Worcesterine sauce
  6. Bring mixture to simmer, stirring and return beef to the pot
  7. Cover and cook for 60 to 90 minutes.

Steak

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp of Olive oil
  • Rosemary
  • 1 small onion chopped
  • 1 tbsp of vineger, Worcesterine sauce and brown sugar
  • 80 ml tomato sauce
  • 50ml Mrs Ball 's chutney
  • Salt, pepper and butter

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pot over low heat
  2. Cook onions until soft
  3. Add sugar, vinegar, tomato sauce, chutney, Worcesterine and bring sauce to boil
  4. Drizzle olive oil into a pan and set over high heat
  5. Rub olive oil on each side of steak and season with salt
  6. place steak on the grill to make cross hatch marks
  7. Add butter, gralic, rosemary and baste on each side
  8. Turn off the heat and allow the steak to finish cooking
