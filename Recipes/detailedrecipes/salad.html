Chakalaka

Ingredients

  • 4 carrots
  • 1 red, green, yellow pepper and onion
  • Hanfdul boiled green beans
  • Cayanne pepper, black pepper, oregano, paprika, curry powder
  • Chicken cubes
  • Nandos and sweet chilli sauce
  • Beans

Instructions

  1. Chop your vegetables
  2. Preheat 2 tbsp of oil
  3. Saute onions until soft
  4. Add vegetables,fry and sweet chilli
  5. In a seperate pot put you stock with water and boil the beans
  6. Add the spices on the vegetables together with cubes and garlic
  7. Add the green beans
  8. Add beans and nandos sauce

Creamy spinach

Ingredients

  • Spinach
  • 2 tbsp of butter
  • 2 tbsp of flour
  • 1 ½ cups of milk/ fresh cream
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Rinse and put the spinach in the pot (do not add water)
  2. Remove from heat after excessive water has drained
  3. In a seperate pot melt butter,once melted add flour
  4. Add milk and whisk until creamy
  5. Add salt, pepper and the spinach
